"TIME + PIECES ©"

Wrsiteys is where Timeless Pieces come together with fashion in perfect harmony. Our journey began with a keen eye for vintage watches that have been maintened through the years. Starting at thrift popups, we quickly saw the demand for these unique finds, leading us to create an online platform where you can easily explore and shop our collection.We've proudly collaborated with Picnic and Thrift and Thriftland Market , allowing us to bring our TimePieces to a broader audience.While we started with vintage watches, we’ve since expanded our offerings to include modern jewelry, ensuring there's something for everyone who appreciates craftsmanship and style. By design of the founders, Wrsiteys is more than just a collection of products—it's a celebration of Time + Pieces that make it memorable. We invite you to discover the story behind each piece in our collection.
